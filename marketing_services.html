Get better results by using on-target marketing tools

Obtain exciting, effective marketing products to promote your professional office or organization. Select a retainer-based service for ongoing marketing needs or choose from the following options:

 

Marketing plan
You wouldn’t build a house without a blueprint, don’t spend money on marketing without a marketing plan. We’ll create a plan that combines your input with our fresh perspective and expertise in reaching the right markets. Plus, we’ll track the results and make adjustments until your goals are met. For a no-cost overview, download the 10 steps to marketing success (tactics & costs).

 

Marketing consulting
Our "professional services" marketing focuses on existing and new market segments. Cezat Creative Resources can provide a detailed analysis of your client base, create a marketing communications plan, and produce image-building materials to achieve desired results. Our niche marketing offers enhanced service and expertise at a fraction of the cost of comprehensive (read: expensive) consulting groups.

 

Website
Make it easy for current and prospective customers to get information and stay connected with your office. Your website should work for you 24/7. We’ll craft your message and work with a web designer to set up a user-friendly format with a design unique to your company or organization. View a sample website for a medical office produced by Cezat Creative Resources and associates.

 

Brochure
Present a professional image of your practice. Allow us to create a brochure specific to your needs. We’ll write it, design it, and handle printing and distribution. [ Brochure sample ]

 

Direct mail
Whether it’s a postcard or letter with a promo item, we’ll see that your message gets to the right audience at the right time to help you achieve desired results. This is ideal for special offers and events.

 

Newsletter
Timely news and tips keep your clients connected. We’re experts at producing lively, informative newsletters. A newsletter showcases your expertise among prospective clients and builds loyalty among existing customers. We’ll also convert it to an online version or PDF for easy access. [ Learn more about starting a newsletter ]

 

Profile card
Present your credentials with flair. More flexible than a brochure and more descriptive than a standard business card, a customized profile card promotes you (and your credentials) as well as your service.
