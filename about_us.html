We can take your next project to a higher level with less hassle — whether it's a printed piece or a website. Quality assurance starts with a streamlined production process that encompasses the following key steps:

  1. Listen to the client. Learn about the project and goals.
  2. Develop a project estimate (based on stated objectives), which is signed by the client before proceeding.
  3. Conduct research and interviews to compile information.
  4. Gather materials (photos, copy, quotes, etc.) to bring the project to life.
  5. Communicate with the client by e-mail, phone and meetings.
  6. Present a great first draft of copy and/or initial design layouts.
  7. Based on client input and revisions, finely tune the copy and design.
  8. Obtain essential approvals, do final proofreading and fact-checking, and compile all project components for pre-launch (web) or pre-flight (print).
  9. Coordinate the printing and/or launch of the project.
  10. Request feedback from clients for continuous process improvement and results tracking.
