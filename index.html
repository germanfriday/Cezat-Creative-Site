Writing, design and strategy for marketing communications

Cezat Creative Resources produces marketing communication materials to reach customers, funders, supporters and prospects. Clients use our services for:

  • Fundraising
  • Sales
  • Marketing for professional offices
  • Referral networks
  • Donor recognition and cultivation
  • Alumni relations
  • Presentations

Our communication vehicles include brochures, newsletters, e-newsletters, websites, annual reports, press releases and media relations, blogs, social media and presentations. We also offer videos and photography.

 

From healthcare systems and universities to professional offices and nonprofit organizations, you can rely on Cezat Creative Resources to handle your marketing communication needs.
